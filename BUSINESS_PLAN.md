## SECTION 02 — THE PROBLEM

Local service businesses hemorrhage revenue not in crises, but in silence:

- A gym in Ingolstadt receives DMs at 23:00 on Sunday. Nobody answers. The lead books the competitor.
- A dental clinic in Lima loses 35% of evening bookings because the receptionist leaves at 18:00.
- A salon in Munich turns away English-speaking customers because the front desk speaks only German.

### The Cost of Silence

| Scenario | Monthly Loss |
|---|---|
| 20 unanswered DMs/month @ €250 avg ticket | **€5,000** |
| 240-min average response time vs. 5-min OPERION | **30–60% conversion gap** |
| One part-time front-desk employee (total cost) | **€1,737/month** |
| OPERION Growth plan (after 25% tax deduction) | **€375/month** |

> **Net savings on a direct replacement basis: €1,362/month.**

This is not a "nice to have." It is a compounding hemorrhage.

| **4 — OPERION** | Done-for-you, trilingual, GDPR-native | **No one else occupies this exact position today.** |

### Defensible moats

1. **GDPR by geography** — Hetzner Frankfurt. US vendors face 18 months of compliance work to enter DACH. OPERION is already there.
2. **Language depth** — DE/EN/ES natively. Not translation. Native generation calibrated per market.
3. **Distribution velocity** — 6 months of content + case studies before a copycat can establish trust.
4. **Done-for-you model** — The target client will never set up a no-code builder. OPERION is the only viable option at their price point.

## SECTION 07 — TECHNOLOGY STACK

| Layer | Phase 0 (Now) | Phase 1 (Month 6+) |
|---|---|---|
| **AI backbone** | Claude (Anthropic) — GDPR DPA signed | Claude + fallback to GPT-4.1 |
| **Runtime** | Python 3.12 + Flask | FastAPI (async, 10× under load) |
| **Database** | SQLite (local) | PostgreSQL on Supabase (multi-tenant, RLS) |
| **Infrastructure** | Local machine | Hetzner VPS, Frankfurt, DE — GDPR by geography |
| **Integration layer** | MCP Protocol | Blotato (social) · Adspirer (ads) · Google (calendar/email) · WhatsApp Business API |

**Portable logic rule:** All business logic lives in plain Python. If Anthropic doubles prices tomorrow, OPERION switches providers in one day. The agents are more durable than any tool they use.
